Who We Are

അറിവ് (meaning "Knowledge" in Malayalam) is a Kerala-based EduTech and career guidance platform dedicated to helping students from Class 11 onwards navigate competitive entrance exams, higher education choices, and career pathways.

We believe that quality guidance should be accessible to every student, regardless of location or financial background — which is why most of our sessions are offered free of charge.

To democratise career guidance and exam preparation for students across Kerala and beyond — bridging the gap between aspiration and achievement through live sessions, digital tools, and expert mentorship.
